My Journey: From Cashew Insights to AI Solutions

A path driven by curiosity, field experience, and a commitment to impactful innovation.

Foundation

Wageningen B.Sc. & Tropical Curiosity

Completed my Bachelor of Science in International Land & Water Management at Wageningen University & Research, fostering a deep curiosity about tropical agriculture, sustainable food systems, and the untapped potential of under-utilized crops.

Immersion

Embrapa Internship, Ceará, Brazil

Journeyed to Ceará for an internship with Embrapa Agroindústria Tropical, the world's leading cashew research center. My goal: understand why the nutritious cashew fruit remained largely neglected compared to the nut.

Discovery

Dual Research Focus: Biochar & Cashew Fruit Barriers

Conducted research on biochar's impact on cashew tree drought resilience and root development. Simultaneously, investigated critical barriers (inefficient supply chains, processing tech, market integration) hindering broader cashew fruit utilization through interviews with farmers and processors.

Realization

The Supply Chain Spark & AI's Potential

The challenges faced by smallholder cashew farmers ignited my interest in supply chain management. Observing global corporations, I recognized the transformative power of efficient logistics and emerging AI technologies to revolutionize agricultural networks.

Ambition

M.Sc. & Collaborative Impact

Transitioning to my Master's, I aim to establish robust international collaborations to co-author research papers at the intersection of policy, supply chain management, and tropical agriculture, driving tangible change in the Global South.

My Unique Edge

I bridge the critical gap between rigorous field science and the transformative power of AI. It's not just about data; it's about actionable intelligence that empowers communities and ecosystems.

Deep Domain Expertise

Wageningen B.Sc. in International Land & Water Management, with immersive Embrapa internship in Ceará, Brazil. Researched biochar's impact on cashew root development and drought resilience, and investigated the systemic neglect of the cashew fruit, fueling a passion for under-utilized crops. Publication on cashew research in progress.

AI & Tech Fluency

Witnessing supply chain inefficiencies first-hand, I embraced a self-driven journey into Python, Machine Learning, LLMs, and GIS. Inspired by global leaders like Unilever and Nestlé, I focus on building practical, AI-powered MVPs to optimize agri-food networks – "Mind and Hand" in action.

Global South Focus & Collaboration

Driven to establish robust international collaborations during my upcoming M.Sc. (2025-27). My goal is to co-create contextually relevant solutions, engage in policy dialogue, and empower smallholder farmers across the Global South through shared research and innovation.

Core Research Pillars

Targeting high-impact publications during my M.Sc. (Wageningen, 2025-27). My research aims to deliver tangible advancements:

AI-Enhanced Phenotyping

Leveraging computer vision and ML for rapid, scalable, and climate-resilient crop trait analysis.

AI-Driven Supply Chain Transformation

Designing intelligent, transparent logistics to overcome market barriers for under-utilized crops (like the cashew fruit), ensuring fair value for smallholders.

Agri-Risk Early Warning

Building predictive models for climate shocks, pests, and diseases to enhance proactive resilience in the Global South.

Policy & Systems Innovation

Leveraging data and AI to co-author research papers and inform policies that foster sustainable practices, enhance food production efficiency, and support economic prosperity in the Global South.
