Key Projects & Innovations

Cashew Biochar Root-Dynamics Trial

Led a five-level biochar experiment on 60+ cashew trees during an Embrapa Ceará internship. Tracked root depth and water-use efficiency via drone imagery and in-situ sensors, developing AI models that unlocked 30% irrigation savings.

SupplyChainAI for Smallholder Cashew

Designed and simulated an AI-powered dashboard predicting shell-in-kernel yields and optimizing freight routes from Ceará, Brazil to Rotterdam. Leveraged AIS shipping feeds and reinforcement learning, achieving an 18% logistics-cost reduction in simulations.

Peer-Reviewed Work & Drafts

Draft: Biochar-Driven Root Plasticity in Cashew (Anacardium occidentale L.) Under Varying Water Regimes

Co-authoring manuscript with Embrapa Agroindústria Tropical. This research quantifies hydraulic redistribution and root architectural changes across four distinct soil depths influenced by five biochar amendment rates, providing critical insights for drought resilience in tropical agroecosystems.
